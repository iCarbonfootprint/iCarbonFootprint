Do you use AC in your home?

Air conditioning

There are around 117 million metric tons of CO2 let out into the air every year from AC's, that's 257,940,846,756 pounds. Three quarters of all homes in the U.S. use air conditioning, so about every home with an AC releases 1064 pounds of CO2 into the air each year

Do you take 10 minute or longer showers?

Showers!

The average shower lasts 8.2 minutes and the average shower head uses 2.1 gallons per minute. Not only that but 80% of men and 90% of women say they shower at least once a day. So it's safe to say that you probably take a shower at least once a day. That would be 365 a year, at that rate it would come to 6,286 gallons, or 56.56 pounds of CO2.
